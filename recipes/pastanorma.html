Pasta alla norma

Ingredients

  • 1 Eggplant
  • 150g Penne
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 400g chopped tomatoes
  • Fresh basil
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Parmesan

Stages

  1. Dice the eggplant into bite-size cubes, then season with salt and pepper then fry in 1tbsp olive oil for 8-10 minutes until golden
  2. In a seperate pan, fry garlic cloves and chili flakes in oil for 1 min until fragrant, then add chopped tomatoes, fresh basil and a pinch of salt. Cook for 5-8 minutes until reduced
  3. Once the pasta is cooked, reserve 1/4 cup of pasta water. Add pasta to the sauce, stir, and add water if too thick. Stir and cook for 1-2 minutes
  4. To serve, season to taste then top with eggplant and parmesan
