Irish Potato Farls

Ingredients

  • 400g cold mash potatoes
  • 1/4 cup plain flour
  • Salt

Stages

  1. Recipe is best using leftover mash, otherwise peel, chop and cook 400g potatoes, then mash and leave until cool enough to handle
  2. Mix potatoes and plain flour in a bowl, then knead until smooth
  3. Divide the mixture into four even balls, then roll one into a circle on a lightly dusted surface. Cut into four triangles.
  4. Cook in a hot pan for 1-2 minutes per side, until lightly golden and crusted
  5. To finish, fry the cooked farl in butter and serve
